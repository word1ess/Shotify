Frequently Asked Questions

Can I view the shoot remotely?
Yes, we can share a live link so you can view images in real time as they’re being taken.
Can I choose the models?
Yes. If we’re producing the shoot, we’ll send you model options within your budget so you can choose the ones you like.
Can you provide custom file naming?
Yes, we offer custom file naming during the shoot for an additional fee.
Will you steam the clothes before the shoot?
Yes, steaming is included in all bookings
How much does it cost?
Please refer to our pricing page for current rates
When will I receive the images?
  • Unedited RAW images (small resolution) are delivered the next day
  • High-resolution files can be mailed to you if you provide a hard drive
How long does retouching take?
2–7 business days , depending on the number of images
How many items can you shoot per day?
  • Half day : Up to 25 items, 3 – 4 photos per item
  • Full day : Up to 50 items, 3 – 4 photos per item
    Includes steaming, styling, basic retouching, and 8 business day delivery.
