Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the pricing?
Pricing starts at $31 per photo for simple eCommerce images with an AI model. For editorial-style photos, pricing begins at $46 per image, while high-end, premium-quality AI editorial photos can go up to $250 per image, depending on style, level of detail, and retouching requirements.
Do you also make product videos with AI models?
Yes, we create all types of product videos — including real product videos, 3D animations, and AI videos featuring your actual products. Contact us to discuss which option works best for your needs and budget.
Any limitations I should know?
Yes, there are a few small limitations. For eCommerce photos, posing options are more limited since we use true photos of the actual product , not processed or generated garments. For fashion editorials, very heavy or highly textured fabrics can sometimes require extra processing. Overall, about 95% of garments are fully compatible, and results maintain 90–100% accuracy in fabric texture and details.
Is AI a good fit for my product photos?
Yes, AI modeling works great for fashion editorials because it offers big savings and fast delivery. It’s also a good choice for eCommerce on-model photos when you have just a few products — since booking a real model for small collections can be costly. For larger collections (30+ items), real models are usually more efficient. AI is also perfect for creating natural environments for any type of product and works very well for Amazon listings.
What’s the difference between AI models for eCommerce vs fashion/editorial?
AI models for eCommerce are perfect for showing your products on a clean background with 100% accuracy . Each garment is photographed on a custom mannequin form , then applied to an AI model without altering the product’s shape or fit.

AI models for fashion or editorial focus on creating a natural environment or styled scene , using AI-generated models and backgrounds. The setting and lighting are designed to reflect your brand’s look. What’s amazing is that we can virtually access locations that are normally restricted or hard to reach and create models based on your exact preferences — from look and pose to style and mood.
How does the process work from shipping products to final images?
You ship your products to us, and we photograph each item in the studio—usually on a ghost mannequin or custom form. We discuss the creative direction with you in advance, using image and model references for guidance. After the photos are ready, our designers apply AI models or environments to create the final images.

We offer AI as an additional service to our ghost mannequin photography. If your products are not photographed by our team, an additional fee may apply for photographing your items before the AI process begins. It’s an efficient system that combines AI and ghost mannequin photography, providing both quality and cost savings.
What references do you need from me (angles, crops, styling)?
Any photo references are helpful. You can share examples of model poses, locations, lighting, or overall mood — anything that shows the look you want. These references help our team match your brand style and deliver images that fit your vision.
Can I choose the model’s look (age, body type, skin tone, hair)?
Yes, you can choose any type of model you prefer — age, body type, skin tone, or hair.
Can you use the same virtual model across the shoot?
Yes, the same AI model can be used throughout the entire shoot to keep all images consistent.
How realistic do the AI models look up close?
That depends on the quality level and your budget. We can create very realistic AI models with true skin texture and fine details , achieving a natural, lifelike look that fits your product and brand style.
Can you match a specific brand vibe or mood board?
Yes, absolutely. Share your mood board or visual references , and we can match the location, model style, lighting, and overall mood to align perfectly with your brand’s look.
