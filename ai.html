Lifestyle Image Integration with AI

Lifestyle Image Integration with AI

We create custom AI lifestyle images by integrating real product photography into virtual environments — with orwithout a model.

Two options:

  • With AI model: Your product is worn by a realistic model placed in a lifestyle or editorial scene (beach, street, studio, etc.)
  • Without model: We create branded product scenes using AI-enhanced environments that match your aesthetic

In both cases, we start by photographing your realproduct — this keeps textures and shape authentic. Then we build custom AI-driven compositions around it. Great for brands needing campaign content without full production costs.

How Does It Work?

How Does It Work?
  1. You ship us the garments
  2. We photograph each piece on a mannequin form
  3. Our team replaces the mannequinwith a realistic AI-generated modelfor fashion or ecommerce,seamlessly wearing your clothing

The garments remain 100% true to the original fit and shape, with no digital manipulation that alters sizing or style. We also provide ghost mannequin photography combined with AI models, making it a cost-effective option for smaller catalogs.
