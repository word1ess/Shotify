What is Flat Lay Photography?

Flat lay photography is a method of arranging products on a flat surface and photographing them from above. It’s an essential style fore-commerce, as it presents items in a clean, organized, and visually appealing way.



At Shootify , we offer and specialize in all types of flat lay photography services for e-commerce brands — including clothing, accessories, beauty products, home goods, and more.

Types of Flat Lay clothing Photograph

At Shootify, we provide a variety of flat lay photography styles

  • Hanging Flat Lays: Garments are hung against a wall or flat surface, ideal for showcasing drape and length. Hanging flat lay photography has become very popular lately among recognizable brands.
  • Tabletop Flat Lays: Products are arranged lying flat on a surface, providing a clean, minimalistic look.
  • Staffed Flat Lays: Forms and shapers are used to give garments more shape, mimicking a 3D effect.
  • Group Styled Flat Lays: Multiple products are styled together to create a dynamic and engaging scene

Retouching for Flat Lays

Retouching is a crucial part of the flat lay photography process:

  • Basic Retouching: Removes all stylist props like pins and clips, ensuring a clean finish.
  • Premium retouching: Includes advanced adjustments, such as removing creases, enhancing symmetry, and improving fabric details for a refined and professional look.

Common Flat Lay Views
(for Clothing)

The most common flat lay shots include:

  • Front view – A clean, straight-on image of the garment, often used as the main product photo
  • Back view – Adds clarity by showing the full design and cut from behind
  • Side view – A close-up of the material to show texture, weave, and color more accurately
  • Detailed shots – Focused images of stitching, buttons, or unique features that add value to your product
