Hat Preparation for Photography

To achieve the best results, please prepare your hats before shipping:

Inspection

Inspect for any dents, marks, or irregular shaping

Condition

Choose hats that are clean, symmetrical, and in perfect condition

Avoid Worn Items

Avoid sending worn, crushed, or misshaped items

Shape Protection

For structured hats, pack them to maintain their shape during shipping

Well-prepared products allow us to create clean, professional images that reflect the quality of your brand
