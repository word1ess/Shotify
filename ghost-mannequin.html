Ghost Mannequin
Photography Service

Ghost mannequin photography is a technique used to create a 3D effect for clothing by photographing garments on a mannequin or form and digitally removing the mannequin during post-production. This highlights the garment's natural shape and structure — making it appear worn, without any distractions.

Why Use a Ghost Mannequin?

This style of photography enhances buyer confidence by showing how clothing fits on a body, which helps boost conversions for e-commerce stores. It’s clean, consistent, and perfect for professional catalogs.

Ghost Mannequin or Flat Lay

When selling clothing online, presentation matters. At Shootify, both Ghost Mannequin Photography and Flat Lay Photography are available to help you create clean, professional product images that drive sales.

Ghost Mannequin Photography

This method uses a mannequin or body form to show the natural fit and shape of your garments. We remove the mannequin in post-production to create a 3D, invisible effect.

  • Helps shoppers see how the item looks when worn
  • Highlights fit, structure, and silhouette
  • Clean, consistent, and ideal for eCommerce and marketplaces like Amazon or Shopify

Flat Lay Photography

Clothing is arranged flat on a table or pinned for a straight-on view. This method creates a two-dimensional look, often usedfor creative styling or basics.

  • Great for highlighting fabric details, textures, and prints
  • A more casual, editorial-friendly style
  • Works well for t-shirts, leggings, swimwear, and kidswear

Do I Need My Ghost Mannequin Photos Retouched?

Yes. Ghost mannequin photography requires retouching as part of the process.

Basic vs. Premium Retouching

Basic Retouching (Included):

  • Removes mannequin parts, clips, and pins
  • Delivers a clean, distraction-free image
  • Ready for use on e-commerce platforms

Premium Retouching (Recommended):

  • Includes all basic retouching
  • Adds symmetry correction
  • Smooths fabric and reduces creases
  • Refines garment shapes for a better fit
  • Enhances the overall appearance for a polished, high-end look

Premium retouching helps your product stand out with a more professional and desirable presentation — perfect for brands aiming for the best online presence.

Common Ghost Mannequin Views

The most popular ghost mannequin shots include:

  • Front view – the primary image for most garments
  • Back view – gives a complete look at the full design
  • Side view – optional, depending on the style andshape

Additional shots can include:

  • Detail photos – to highlight stitching, fabric texture,buttons, or other features
  • Fabric swatch – great for showing fabric and color up close

We’ll work with you to determine the best angles and shots based on your product and goals.
