Frequently Asked Questions

What is a 360° product video?
A 360° product video is a rotating clip that shows the product from all angles. Unlike still images, it allows customers to visually inspect every side—ideal for increasing buyer confidence. It’s part of our 360 degree product photography offering and works well on Amazon, Shopify, and brand websites.
What’s the difference between a 360 spin and an eCommerce video?
A 360 spin video shows the product rotating in place. An ecommerce video includes product interaction, movement, or demonstrations—such as opening packaging, showing features, or applying beauty products.
Can you create videos with models wearing or using the product?
Yes! We offer ecommerce product videos with a model to show fit, movement, or application. These are perfect for fashion, jewelry, skincare, and other lifestyle-focused brands.
Are the videos ready for Amazon or Shopify?
Yes, all our ecommerce videos are delivered in platform-friendly formats and resolutions optimized for online stores, marketplaces, and social media.
Can I combine product photography and video in one shoot?
Yes. We often combine still photos and video in a single session—especially for fashion shoots. This allows us to capture clean eCommerce images of clothing along with video to show how the product moves on a model. It's an efficient way to get everything you need with consistent lighting and styling.
