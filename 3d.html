Frequently Asked Questions

What is 3D product modeling for eCommerce?
3D product modeling involves creating a digital version of a product that can be used for interactive 360 views, product animations, or photorealistic renders. It improves online shopping experiences by letting buyers inspect products from all angles.
What’s the difference between 3D rendering and 3D animation?
3D rendering produces still images from a digital model. 3D animation creates a video or interactive element that moves—like a rotating shoe or a zoom-in feature showing product texture and function.
Can I use 3D models on Amazon or Shopify?
Yes! Our 3D product visualization and 360-degree product spin videos are optimized for Amazon listings, Shopify stores, and other eCommerce platforms.
Do I need to send a physical product?
We prefer to work with the physical product so we can take accurate measurements and analyze texture, material, and finish. But if the product isn’t available, we can still create a 3D model using CAD files, reference images, or detailed drawings
How long does 3D animation take?
Turnaround depends on complexity. Simple 360° spins take 5–10 days. Advanced animations with zoom, text, and effects may take longer.
Can I get photos from my 3D model?
Yes, we offer product 3D rendering services that generate high-res images from 3D files—perfect for eCommerce listings, ads, and packaging.
