Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get an annual membership?
You can purchase an annual membership for $1200 per year. Membership provides exclusive benefits, including discounts and priority services. Contact us through our website or email to get started.
How can I pay?
We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For large projects or memberships, payment plans can also be arranged upon request.
Can I get a free test photo?
Yes, we offer a free test photo to ensure it matches your image reference and expectations
What is the difference between Basic and Premium retouching?
Basic retouching includes removing dust, scratches, and styling props. Premium retouching involves detailed work, such as crease removal and enhancing symmetry for an overall better look of the product.
What is the process?
Contact us to discuss your project, desired results, files, and image specifications. Once aligned, you will receive a final quote. After shipping your product to us, we will create the final images. For large volume orders, we offer a test photo for approval before completing the entire project.
Do I get my samples back?
Yes. Please provide a shipping label, or we can purchase one and include it in your invoice. If you prefer us to utilize a sample, we can do so. Samples are stored for up to two weeks. For longer storage, additional fees apply.
How do I receive my images?
We deliver images digitally via a secure download link. Images are stored for up to two months. After that, they will be deleted unless you opt for extended storage at a monthly fee. Restoring deleted files costs $250, and after one year, restoration is no longer available.
What image sizes and formats are available?
We can work based on your image and size specifications or provide full-size JPEGs. Additional resizing or format conversions are available for $1 per format and $2 per size.
Can I request custom file naming?
Yes, custom file naming is available for $1 per photo.
Are additional backgrounds or translucent background options available?
Yes, we can provide a background of your choice or a transparent background in addition to the original file for $3 per photo.
What does styling include?
Styling involves shaping and positioning the product to achieve an appealing look, while maintaining consistency across similar products within the same group for a cohesive presentation. All e-commerce style photos include a stylist in the price. For editorial or creatively styled photos, additional fees may apply.
What is the difference between flat lay basic and flat lay advanced?
Basic flat lay styling is suitable for simple products like T-shirts, denim, and kids' clothes, styled in a straightforward flat lay. Advanced flat lay involves complex products such as jackets and coats or items requiring intricate styling. This includes using shapers to create more volume or a 3D look. Products requiring longer styling and steaming times are also categorized as advanced.
What is flat lay group?
Flat lay group refers to styled arrangements of multiple products in a single image, offering a cohesive presentation for related items
What is the difference between fashion shoes and basic shoes photography?
  1. Simple: Small, non-reflective items that hold their shape, such as cups, candles, or packaging.
  2. Medium: Medium-sized items requiring some styling or with partially reflective surfaces, like soft packaging.
  3. Complex: Large or highly reflective items needing additional propping or styling to maintain their shape.
What does the production fee cover?
You can purchase an annual membership for $1200 per year. Membership provides exclusive benefits, including discounts and priority services. Contact us through our website or email to get started.
What is the cost of hiring models?
Model costs vary based on representation and experience:
  • Non-agency represented models: $400 for a half day, $1000 for a full day.
  • Agency-represented models: Starting rates of $600 for a half day, $1200 for a full day. Higher budgets provide access to a wider range of models, including premium options.
Do you offer photo coloring?
Yes, we can create additional colors in post-production. To get the best result, we photograph light-colored items to create additional light shades, medium tones for medium shades, and dark items for dark shades. Creating light shades from dark or reverse may not look as natural. Additional colors are $10 per photo
