Product Preparation for Photography

To ensure the best results with your Shootify product photography session, please follow these guidelines:

Product Inspection

Inspect all items for damage, wrinkles, or defects before shipping. Please send us the cleanest, photo-ready pieces .

Duplicates Recommended

For certain items, we recommend sending two of each , in case one needs adjustment or backup during the shoot.

Shootify’s Process

Upon arrival, we unpack and prepare each item. Tags may be removed if visible, and styling forms or shapers may be used to enhance garment presentation

Note: Proper preparation helps us deliver polished, professional images that stay consistent across your product catalog.
