Shoe Preparation for Photography

To get the best results from your shoot, please prepare your footwear before sending:

Inspection

Inspect for any scuffs, marks, or factory defects

Selectionn

Select the cleanest, most symmetrical shoes

Cleanliness

Make sure shoes are unworn and free of dust,smudges, or fingerprints

Shape Support

Stuff shoes with tissue or padding to help them hold shape

Proper preparation ensures polished, professional images that reflect the quality of your brand.
