Product Preparation for Supplements & Vitamins Product Photography

To ensure the best results for your supplement product shoot, please follow these preparation guidelines before sending your items:

Condition

Send unused, undamaged, and cleanproducts

Labeling

Make sure labels are sharp, centered, straight, and fully adhered (no peeling or bubbling)

Seals & Closures

Ensure safety seals, caps, or closures are intact and clean

Packaging Integrity

Avoid sending dented bottles, scratched lids, or faded packaging

Backup Quantity

We recommend sending two of each product in case a backup is needed for styling or retouching

Proper preparation allows us to produce polished, high-quality images that match your brand and meet online marketplace standards.
