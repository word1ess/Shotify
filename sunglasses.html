Eyewear Preparation for Photography

To get the best results from your shoot, please prepare your products before sending:

Inspection

Inspect for any scratches, discoloration, or factory defects

Selection

Select the cleanest, most symmetrical items

Cleanliness

Make sure products are unworn and free of fingerprints or smudges

Condition

Do not send broken, bent, or damaged frames

Proper preparation ensures polished, professional images that reflect the quality of your brand.
