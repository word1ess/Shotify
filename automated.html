Automated Low-Cost Shoe Photography

Automated shoe photography is a simple, affordable solution for brands that need clean and consistent product images at scale. We use professional lighting systems and advanced software to automate the process — making it faster, cost-effective, and ideal for high-volumeneeds.

Product Requirements:

  • Minimum order: 150 photos
  • Product is non-reflective and non-translucent
  • Holds natural shape (no need for styling or shaping)
  • Size must be under 17 x 17 x 17 inches
  • No custom lighting or advanced setups required

Pricing:

  • 150–300 photos: $11 per image including basic retouching
  • 300+ photos: $9 per image

Most popular shoe angles:

While there are many ways to photograph shoes, the most common setups include 5–7 images per style. These usually cover:

  • Side view (or both sides)
  • Front view
  • Back view
  • 45-degree angle
  • Top view
  • Bottom sole view
  • Additional close-ups (branding, texture, stitching)

Shoes can be photographed as a single shoe or as a pair,depending on client preferences and usage.

Shoe Preparation for Photography

To get the best results from your shoot, please prepare your footwear before sending:

Inspection

Inspect for any scuffs, marks, or factory defects

Selectionn

Select the cleanest, most symmetrical shoes

Cleanliness

Make sure shoes are unworn and free of dust,smudges, or fingerprints

Shape Support

Stuff shoes with tissue or padding to help them hold shape

Proper preparation ensures polished, professional images that reflect the quality of your brand.
