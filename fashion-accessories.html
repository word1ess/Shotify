Fashion Accessories Preparation for Photography

To ensure the best possible results from your shoot, please follow these guidelines when preparing fashion accessories for product photography:

Clothing Inspection

Inspect each item for defects,
scratches, or signs of wear

Choice

Choose clean, undamaged,
and symmetrical products

Condition

Make sure straps, buckles,
and fastenings are secure and in place

Proper preparation helps us create polished, high-quality images that match your brand and meet eCommerce standards.
