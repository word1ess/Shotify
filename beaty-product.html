Product Preparation for Beauty & Cosmetic Photography

To ensure the best results, please follow these guidelines before sending your products:

Condition

Use only unused, clean, and undamaged items

Labeling

Make sure labels are printed sharply, centered, and applied evenly (no peeling or bubbles)

Closures

Ensure pumps, caps, and seals are intact and clean

Packaging

Avoid dented, scratched, or squeezed tubes and jars

Quantity

We recommend sending two of each productto ensure we have a backup for styling or retouching if needed

Proper prep helps us createsharp, professional images that meet eCommerce standards and reflect the quality of your brand.
