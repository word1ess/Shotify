Product Preparation for Photography

To ensure the best results with your Shootify product photography session, please follow these guidelines:

Product Inspection

Inspect all items for damage, wrinkles, or defects before shipping. Please send us the cleanest, photo-ready pieces.

Duplicates Recommended

For certain items, we recommend sending two of each , in case one needs adjustment or backup during the shoot

Shootify’s Process

Upon arrival, we unpack and prepare each item. Tags may be removed if visible, and styling forms or shapers may be used to enhance garment presentation.

Note: Proper preparation helps us deliver polished, professional images that stay consistent across your product catalog.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of images do I need for Amazon?
  • Hero/main image on white
  • Lifestyle images
  • Infographic images
  • Product details and close-ups
  • Packaging shots
Do you follow Amazon image requirements?
Yes: white background (RGB 255,255,255), 85% product fill, high resolution (1000x1000+ px), n o borders, watermarks, or logos
What’s included in your Amazon photography service?
Studio photography, lighting, retouching, image formatting, and optional lifestyle or infographic design
Can I get lifestyle or infographic images too?
Yes, many clients want full sets: hero, lifestyle, close-ups, and infographics
How much does it cost?
Pricing depends on the number of images and type of product. Most clients want 5–7 images per listing.
How quickly can I get the photos?
Turnaround is typically 5 – 7 business days. Rush options may be available
Can you photograph small or complex products?
Yes, from supplements and electronics to fashion and beauty — we adapt to the product type.
Do I have to be local to use your services?
No. Just ship your product to the studio. Everything is handled remotely.
